Hi, I'm Prahalad Belavadi.

I'm a self taught developer.

I'm fascinated by, work on and research on applied cryptography.

I also build Websites for people using the mean stack as a swiss knife

I write technical articles on stuff that I find are quite simple and once found insanely hard to grasp.

You can find my writings on Medium and on Quora.

You can find earlier code I've written at Github and on Gitlab.

I do enjoy reading books of depth, watching documentaries, eating food(v), playing football and daytime travel(without traffic preferably). Unraveling through the breadths of the internet(archives) is a guilty pleasure, I'd say.

If you would like to get in touch with me, whether it be for tech support, work, or to just say hi, feel free to send me an email or a tweet.
